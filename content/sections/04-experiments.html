IV. Experiments

The experiments test which changes actually improve probabilistic geolocalization. Caption and prompting variants were evaluated first, but the final presentation reports that adding text captions did not improve the model in these experiments.

The largest improvement comes from replacing global spherical Riemannian flow matching with a local R2 flow-matching setup. Adding a classifier prior then improves region-level canton accuracy without worsening the distance or entropy metrics.
