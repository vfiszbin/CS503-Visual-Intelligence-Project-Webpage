III. Methodology

The project compares deterministic and probabilistic models. The regression baseline maps a StreetCLIP image embedding directly to latitude and longitude. The flow-matching variants instead sample many trajectories and aggregate their endpoints into a predicted spatial distribution.

The initial generative baseline used a spherical S2 formulation. The final presentation shows that replacing this with a local R2 Euclidean formulation is the most important architectural change: Switzerland is geographically small enough that the flat local approximation gives a better inductive bias for this task.

The final tuned R2 model uses CFG=0, LayerNorm on the conditioning signal, and 100 training epochs. A later variant concatenates a classifier-derived canton prior with the visual embedding before conditioning the flow model.
